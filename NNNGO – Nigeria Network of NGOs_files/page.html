To commemorate World Day of Social Justice, Legend Golden Care Foundation held a one-day Advocacy Workshop on Non-coercive Interrogation and Torture Prevention in Nigeria - themed: Enhancing Law Enforcement Practices in Nigeria.

The Torture Prevention project, which Legend Golden Care Foundation, funded by European Union through the International Rehabilitation Council for Torture Victims has been actively creating widespread awareness about the dangers of torture and the im...portance of adopting humane interrogation methods, as well as sustaining and strengthening holistic rehabilitation for torture survivors.
Through community engagements, outreach programs, and advocacy efforts, Legend Golden Care Foundation has reached various communities, particularly across Abuja, the nation's capital.

This initiative aligns with Legend Golden Care’s commitment to advancing social justice and protecting human dignity in Nigeria.

Learn more about their operations at: https://legendcare.org/
